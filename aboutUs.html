About Tail to Heart

Tail to Heart originated from a small group of passionate animal lovers who started rescuing stray dogs and cats in their local neighbourhood. What began as informal rescues from personal garages quickly developed into an organised effort, boosted by community donations and social media outreach.

Our target audience includes families seeking pets, donors, schools, volunteers, and community members needing affordable vet services.
