Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption process?

Our process includes an application, home visit, and meeting with the pet to ensure a good match.

How much does adoption cost?

Adoption fees range from R500-R1500 depending on the pet, which covers vaccinations and spay/neuter.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

Yes, we have a lifetime return policy. We want every pet to have the right home.

Do you offer low-cost vet services?

Yes, we provide discounted vaccinations and spay/neuter services to the community.
